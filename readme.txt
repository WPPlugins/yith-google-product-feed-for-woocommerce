YITH Google Product Feed for WooCommerce allows you to generate product feeds and sync your products with your Google Shopping merchant center

== Description ==

Do you know Google Merchant, the great tool by Google, well-conceived for those who aim to boost their sales?
If you have a WooCommerce-based store and would like to showcase your products on Google Merchant too, “YITH Google Product Feed for WooCommerce”
plugin will be your best friend: it’s easy as pie. Just activate the plugin and create a feed file including the products you have selected; upload
 it on Google Merchant and you'll be able to show your products on Google search engine in no time.

= Features: =

* Create a feed with five simple product to sync your products with your Google Shopping merchant center

= PREMIUM FEATURES of YITH Google Product Feed for WooCommerce: =

* Create multiple feeds
* Create different feeds in .xml and .txt format.
* View the list of feeds with the related URL.
* Edit the general settings for each single product.
* Filter products in the feed (by categories, by tag, manual product selection, manual product exclusion)
* Associate any field of WooCommerce products to Google Merchant fields.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
2. Upload the plugin folder into the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate `YITH Google Product Feed for WooCommerce` from Plugins page.

YITH Google Product Feed for WooCommerce will add a new submenu called "Google Product Feed" in "YITH Plugins" menu. From this page you will able to
configure all the plugin settings.
